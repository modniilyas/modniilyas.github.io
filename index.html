About

I am a Ph.D. Candidate at the Mohammadia School of Engineering (EMI) in Rabat, Morocco. My research focuses on the intersection of Machine Learning, Optimization, and Road Safety, with a specific interest in Decision Trees, Explainable AI (XAI), and Large Language Models (LLMs).

My thesis, "Design and Development of an Intelligent System for Road Safety Data Valorization," aims to develop actionable predictive models to identify accident risk factors. I am passionate about bridging the gap between theoretical AI and real-world application to enhance public safety.
